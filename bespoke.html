The Art of the
Crafted Silhouette

The KUHI bespoke experience is a meticulous three-stage evolution, ensuring every thread aligns with your essence.

01

The Consultation

A private session to explore textures, color palettes, and silhouettes. We discuss the occasion, your personal style, and the rich history of the weaves you choose.

02

The Toile & Fitting

We create a prototype 'toile' to perfect the fit. This architectural stage ensures the drape is impeccable before the final silk is ever touched by a needle.

03

The Final Loom

Your garment is hand-woven by our master artisans. The final result is a masterpiece of Assamese couture, uniquely yours.
